★ FAQs

Frequently asked questions

If you still have any doubts about hair transplant, dental treatment, skin care or appointments, these answers might help. You can always call or WhatsApp us for more details.

Is hair transplant permanent? How long do results last? +

Transplanted hair is usually taken from the permanent “donor area” and is designed to last many years. However, overall hair health also depends on genetics, lifestyle and medical conditions. We guide you on medicines, PRP / GFC and long-term care to maintain results.

Is hair transplant or PRP very painful? +

Most patients describe it as mild discomfort, not severe pain. Local anaesthesia is used during hair transplant and numbing creams / injections for PRP or GFC. We also guide you with pain medicines and aftercare so you stay comfortable.

How many sittings are needed for dental RCT, implants or braces? +

It depends on your case. Many root canals can be done in 1–2 visits. Implants usually need multiple stages for best healing. Braces / aligners treatment can take several months to years. During consultation, we give you a personalised plan with timelines.

Do you offer EMI or flexible payment options? +

For higher-value treatments like hair transplant, smile makeover or multiple procedures, we can guide you with staged payments and available offers. Please discuss this during consultation or call our reception for current options.

How do I share my reports or photos before visiting? +

You can WhatsApp your scalp photos, dental X-rays or skin pictures to our clinic number and our team will help you with basic guidance and appointment slot suggestions. Final treatment plan is decided only after physical consultation.

Do I need to take an appointment or can I walk in directly? +

Walk-ins are welcome, but to avoid waiting and to ensure the right doctor is available, we recommend booking an appointment by call, WhatsApp or the form above – especially for hair transplant and longer dental procedures.
