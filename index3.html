Bihar Police Driver Constable 2025
Overview

The Bihar Police Department has officially released the Bihar Police Driver Constable Recruitment 2025 notification for 4,361 posts. Eligible candidates can apply online through the official website. This recruitment drive aims to strengthen law enforcement by inducting qualified drivers into the Bihar Police force.

Important Dates
  • Application Start Date: 21st July 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 20th August 2025
  • Admit Card Release: To be announced
  • Exam Date: Tentatively in September 2025
Application Fee
  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹675
  • SC / ST / Female (Bihar): ₹180
  • Payment Mode: Online via Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card
Eligibility Criteria
  • Must have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) from a recognized board.
  • Must possess a valid LMV/HMV Driving License.
  • Should fulfill physical standards set by CSBC Bihar.
Age Limit
  • General Category: 20 to 25 years
  • OBC / EWS: 20 to 27 years
  • SC / ST: 20 to 30 years
  • Age relaxation applicable as per Bihar Govt. rules.
Category-wise Post Distribution

The total 4,361 vacancies are divided among General, OBC, SC, ST, and EWS candidates. For detailed category-wise distribution, refer to the official notification PDF.

Work Profile & Responsibilities

Below are the key duties and responsibilities expected from a Driver Constable in Bihar Police:

Duty Description
Vehicle Operation Drive official police vehicles for daily patrols, emergency calls, and transport duties.
Transport Personnel Ensure safe and timely transportation of police officers and staff to required locations.
Vehicle Maintenance Perform basic maintenance checks and report issues for timely repairs and servicing.
Emergency Response Assist in rapid response during emergencies and coordinate quick transportation of personnel or victims.
Record Keeping Maintain daily logbooks of vehicle usage, fuel consumption, and movements as per department protocol.
Support Duties Help during traffic management, VIP movements, and other police operations when required.

Note: Duties may vary slightly depending on location, department needs, and emergency situations.
